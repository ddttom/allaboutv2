# Invisible Users

Content about invisible users and accessibility.

## Concept

"Invisible users" are the users traditional UX doesn't design for:

- AI agents reading your content
- Screen reader users
- Automated crawlers
- Assistive technology users

MX makes content work for these invisible users.
