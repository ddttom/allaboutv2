# Media219 Ltd

**Television Graphics Studio — 30+ Years of Broadcast Excellence**

## Who We Are

Media219 Ltd creates television graphics and title sequences for broadcasters worldwide. With over three decades in the industry, we specialise in sports broadcasting graphics — from Premier League match packages to FIFA World Cup player profiles.

## What We Do

### Television Graphics

Broadcast-quality graphics packages for live television. Real-time data integration, clean visual language, instant viewer comprehension.

### Title Sequences

Opening titles that set the tone. Programme branding that viewers remember. From the first frame, audiences know what they're watching.

### 3D Models

Stadium fly-throughs. Virtual environments. Animated elements that bring statistics to life. If it needs depth, we build it.

### Virtual Sets

Complete virtual studio environments. From news desks to sports analysis platforms. Flexible, scalable, broadcast-ready.
