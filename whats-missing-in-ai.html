What's Missing from AI

Critical Gaps and Corporate Solutions

The Fundamental Flaws in Modern AI

Despite impressive headlines about AI capabilities, significant limitations continue to hinder their potential and create risks for organizations.

The Hallucination Problem

  • AI systems generate information that appears factual but is fabricated
  • They provide answers with confidence rather than acknowledging limitations
  • This isn't an occasional glitchâ€”it's in their fundamental design
  • Particularly dangerous in specialized domains like law, medicine, and finance

Toxic Training Data

  • Most large language models are trained on internet data
  • This includes misinformation, extremism, and various biases
  • Models have ingested fiction, propaganda, and falsehoods
  • Even with filtering, comprehensive curation is practically impossible

The Memory Problem

  • AI systems lack persistent memory across interactions
  • Each conversation starts fresh with minimal retention
  • Systems can contradict themselves across sessions
  • Prevents building institutional knowledge and relationships

Knowledge Cutoff Limitations

  • AI systems have binary knowledge boundaries
  • Understanding stops at a specific cutoff date
  • Systems confidently respond based on potentially outdated information
  • Particularly problematic for rapidly evolving fields

Cultural Alignment Biases

  • Most AI systems reflect Western, English-speaking perspectives
  • Alternative models demonstrate different cultural alignments
  • Systems interpret information through specific cultural lenses
  • Organizations outside dominant cultures risk misalignment with their values

Regulatory Considerations

  • Data Privacy Frameworks (GDPR, CCPA)
  • Sector-Specific Regulations
  • Financial services (model explainability)
  • Healthcare (HIPAA compliance)
  • Legal services (professional responsibility)
  • Emerging AI-Specific Regulations

Solutions: Structured Data Approaches

  • JSON-LD: Creating machine-readable context
  • The emerging llms.txt standard (like robots.txt for AI)
  • Building AI-friendly information architecture
  • Creating explicit bridges between human content and machine understanding
