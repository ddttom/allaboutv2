# Apfelwein Wagner

A Frankfurt institution since 1931. Three generations of the Wagner family have served
Hessian cuisine and handcrafted apple wine in the Sachsenhausen quarter.

## The menu

Traditional Hessian fare. The Grüne Soße is the signature — seven fresh herbs mixed into
a cold sauce, served with boiled eggs and potatoes. Vegetarian by nature, local by tradition.

The Handkäs mit Musik is an acquired taste — tangy sour milk cheese marinated in vinegar
and onions. "Musik" refers to the digestive sounds that follow.

House apple wine is pressed on-site from local apples. Served in the traditional Bembel
(stoneware jug) and poured into ridged glasses.
