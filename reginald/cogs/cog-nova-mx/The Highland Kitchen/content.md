## Welcome to The Highland Kitchen

We believe the best meals start with the best ingredients. Every dish on our menu is crafted from produce sourced directly from Highland farms, with our menu evolving alongside the seasons.

## What We Offer

- **Seasonal Scottish cuisine** — our menu changes quarterly
- **Local sourcing** — all meat, fish, and vegetables from within 50 miles
- **Dietary accommodations** — vegetarian, gluten-free, and dairy-free options always available
- **Scottish wine and spirits** — featuring local distilleries and vineyards

## Today's Highlights

Our chef recommends the **Pan-seared Salmon** with seasonal vegetables, and the **Wild Mushroom Risotto** for vegetarian diners. Finish with our legendary **Cranachan** — a Highland classic with raspberries, cream, oats, and whisky.

## Booking

We recommend booking for dinner, especially Friday and Saturday. Walk-ins welcome for lunch.
