# CMS Summit 2026

The annual gathering of content management professionals, hosted by Boye & Company
in Frankfurt am Main. Single-track format — every attendee sees every talk.

## Why this cog matters

When a conference attendee scans the event QR code, their personal cogs determine
what they see:

- **Consultant Tom** sees the speaker schedule, networking opportunities, and session topics
  that match his professional tags (CMS, content strategy, digital transformation)
- **Base Tom** sees general event info — date, location, format
- **Any attendee** with professional cogs gets personalised session recommendations
  based on their expertise and interests

## The talk

Tom Cranstoun presents "The Machine Experience" — a live demonstration of MX OS.
Three phones, three identities, one restaurant QR code. The audience watches the
same cog deliver three different experiences in real time.

The punchline: you wrote the instructions. The machine followed them. No AI hallucination.
No black box. Just your cogs, readable by every machine on earth.
