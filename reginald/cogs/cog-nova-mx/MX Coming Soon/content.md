# MX — Your Business, Readable by Every Machine on Earth

AI agents are reading the web and getting it wrong. A river cruise costs two thousand pounds — the agent reads two hundred and three thousand. A support article explains the fix — the agent says no fix exists. Every wrong answer wastes electricity, loses sales, erodes trust.

Machine Experience (MX) fixes this. It makes your content readable by every AI agent on earth — without making it less readable by humans. The metadata is for machines. The prose is for people. Same content, both audiences served.

## About the Author

Tom Cranstoun has been shaping the technology industry for over 40 years.

Starting at 27, he built products and systems used by millions. A long-standing member of the CMS Experts community, he has worked with organisations including Nissan, Ford, Jaguar Land Rover, and Twitter/X.

In 2024, Tom published a [widely read piece on CMS Critic](https://cmscritic.com/a-cms-consultants-takeaways-from-cms-kickoff-2024) identifying what he called the AI tipping point — the moment when designing for machines became as important as designing for humans. That insight became the foundation for the Machine Experience community he founded, and for this book. His work spans content strategy, AI integration, and the design of systems that remain readable and useful regardless of who — or what — is consuming them. MX: The Handbook is the distillation of that thinking into a practical guide for anyone building or managing digital experiences today.
