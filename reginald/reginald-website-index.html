What is MX?

MX (Machine Experience) is the practice of adding metadata and instructions to internet content so AI agents don't have to think. When AI must infer from incomplete context, it hallucinates. Explicit structure prevents this.

MX is not SEO. MX is not accessibility. MX is the discipline of making documentation machine-readable — so AI systems can find, parse, and trust it.

REGINALD is the product that makes MX operational at scale: the registry where MX-compliant documents live, queryable by any AI system.
