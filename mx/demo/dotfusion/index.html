MX-Ready: Built for Humans and AI Agents

This demo shows what Dotfusion's site looks like with Machine Experience (MX) best practices applied

Semantic HTML

Proper <nav>, <main>, <header>, and <footer> landmarks. AI agents can understand and navigate the site structure.

Schema.org Data

Organization, BreadcrumbList, and Service structured data. AI agents can extract business information without parsing HTML.

WCAG 2.1 AA

Skip links, proper form labels, heading hierarchy, focus indicators, and ARIA attributes. Accessible to all users and agents.

Open Graph & Twitter

Rich previews on LinkedIn, Slack, and social platforms. AI platforms can generate meaningful summaries.

llms.txt

Machine-readable service descriptions at /llms.txt. AI assistants can recommend Dotfusion to prospects.

Performance

Zero inline styles, lazy-loaded images, proper dimensions. Sub-3-second load times instead of 18+ seconds.
