For over 25 years, Dotfusion has empowered organizations across Canada and globally to achieve digital transformation, delivering headless CMS, mobile apps, eCommerce, and AI solutions.

Our Story

Dotfusion was founded in 1998 in Toronto with a simple mission: help organizations build better digital experiences. We were among the first agencies in Canada to adopt headless CMS architecture, long before it became an industry standard.

Today we are a 24-person team across Toronto and Montreal, working with Fortune 500 brands including Oxford Properties, Mitsubishi Electric, Intuit, and Cirque du Soleil. Our 14-year partnership with Brookfield Properties stands as proof that we build relationships that last.

As a certified B Corporation, we hold ourselves to higher standards. Growth should contribute positively to people, communities, and the planet. This commitment extends to our work: we build accessible, performant, sustainable digital products.

Our AI & Automation Centre of Excellence represents the next chapter. From chatbots to Answer Engine Optimization, we help clients prepare for a world where AI agents are primary users of their digital properties.
