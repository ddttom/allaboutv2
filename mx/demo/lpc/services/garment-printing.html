What We Print

LPC Design + Print offers custom printing on a wide range of garments and accessories. Whether you're a business looking for branded workwear, a sports club needing team shirts, or an individual wanting a personalized gift, we can help.

T-Shirts

Custom printed t-shirts in any quantity, from single items to bulk orders. All sizes and styles available.

Hoodies & Sweatshirts

Personalized hoodies and sweatshirts for teams, events, or casual wear.

Polo Shirts

Professional polo shirts with your company logo or design.

Gilets & Vests

Custom gilets for outdoor events, corporate wear, or promotional use.

Bags & Totes

Branded bags and tote bags for giveaways, events, or retail.

Other Items

Ask us about caps, aprons, and other custom garment options.

How It Works

  1. Choose Your Garment: Select from our range of t-shirts, hoodies, polo shirts, bags, and more.
  2. Provide Your Design: Send us your logo, artwork, or text. Don't have a design? We can help create one.
  3. Select Quantity: From one item to hundreds—there's no minimum order.
  4. Get a Quote: We'll provide pricing and confirm the details with you.
  5. We Print & Deliver: Fast turnaround, high quality, ready when you need it.
