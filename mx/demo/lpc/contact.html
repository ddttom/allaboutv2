Visit Us

We're located on Main Street in Largs, tucked down a side street at the top end. Nearby car parks make it easy to stop by with your materials or to collect finished work.

Directions:

  • From Glasgow: A78 coastal road (approximately 45 minutes)
  • Local parking: Multiple car parks within walking distance
  • By train: Largs station is a short walk from our shop
