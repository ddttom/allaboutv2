About LPC Design + Print

Family-run printing excellence since 1969

Our Story

LPC Design + Print has been serving Largs, Scotland, and beyond since 1969—over 50 years of printing excellence. What started as a traditional printing company has evolved to embrace modern design and digital services, while maintaining the personal touch and quality craftsmanship that defined our earliest days.

We're a family-run business, and that means something important to us: every customer matters, every project gets our full attention, and we take pride in what we deliver. Whether you need a single business card or a thousand, we handle your job with the same care and professionalism.

What Makes Us Different

No Minimum Order

Many printing companies require minimum quantities. We don't. Need just one t-shirt printed? We'll do it. One poster? No problem. This flexibility makes us the go-to choice for individuals, small businesses, and anyone who needs quality printing without the pressure of large order minimums.

50+ Years Experience

Since 1969, we've seen printing technology evolve from hot metal typesetting to digital printing and beyond. This experience means we understand not just how to print, but what works—the designs that communicate effectively, the materials that last, the finishes that impress.

Personal Service

When you work with LPC, you work directly with Scott McGregor and Stuart McGregor. No call centers, no automated systems—just real people who know your name, understand your project, and care about getting it right. Many of our customers have been with us for years, and that's no accident.

Fast Turnaround

We're known for handling rush jobs efficiently. Our customers have told us stories of 15-minute turnarounds, same-day service, and last-minute rescues. When you need it fast, we deliver—without compromising quality.

Meet the Team

Scott McGregor

Owner

Scott leads LPC Design + Print with a commitment to quality and customer service that has earned the company a 100% recommendation rate across 20 reviews. His hands-on approach means customers work directly with someone who has the authority to make decisions and the expertise to deliver results.

Stuart McGregor

Designer

Stuart McGregor brings creative talent to every project, from designing fresh, lively websites to solving technical problems that have stumped others. Customers consistently praise his design skills, his willingness to help with "dodgy design skills," and his ability to turn ideas into reality.

Community Involvement

We're proud to be part of the Largs community. Over the years, we've supported local charities, printed materials for community events, and helped countless local businesses establish their professional presence. When local organizations need help, we're there—because supporting our community is part of who we are.
