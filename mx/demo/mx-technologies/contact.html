Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you start?

Depends on current workload and your timeline. Discovery calls typically happen within a week. Formal engagements usually start 2-4 weeks after proposal acceptance.

Do you work with companies outside [region]?

Yes. We work with organizations globally. Time zone differences have never been an obstacle for quality collaboration.

What if we have an urgent need?

Contact us directly at urgent@mxtechnologies.com with “URGENT” in the subject line. We’ll prioritize review and response for time-sensitive situations.

Can we start with a small engagement first?

Absolutely. Many clients start with an MX Assessment or focused training workshop before committing to larger implementations. We recommend starting small and expanding as you see results.

Do you sign NDAs?

Yes. We routinely sign NDAs and work under client confidentiality agreements. Your business information stays private.

What if we’re not sure MX is right for us?

That’s what the discovery call is for. We’ll be honest about whether MX is appropriate for your situation. Sometimes the answer is “not yet” or “focus on other priorities first.” We’d rather build long-term relationships than force-fit solutions.

About Response Times

We aim for:

  • 24-hour response to contact form submissions (business days)
  • 12-hour response to emails marked urgent
  • Same-day response to existing client inquiries

Realistic expectations:

  • Complex inquiries may require additional research before responding
  • Detailed proposals typically take 3-5 business days after discovery call
  • Holiday periods and conferences may add 1-2 days to response times

We prioritize quality responses over fast responses. You’ll get a thoughtful, personalized reply—not a template.

A Note on AI Agents

This contact page practices what we preach.
