## What is Machine Experience (MX)?

MX is the discipline of designing digital systems so that machines can read, trust, and act — reliably, safely and consistently — across channels, devices and emerging agent platforms.

AI agents are becoming a new consumption layer for enterprise digital platforms — interpreting, transforming, comparing and acting on information on behalf of users, teams and systems. That changes what "quality" means. Not just user experience.
